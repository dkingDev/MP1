We have some locations across the United Kingdom for you to choose from.

Most well-known ski resorts in the UK include Cairngorm Mountain in Scotland, Glenshee Ski Centre also in Scotland, and The Snow Centre in Hemel Hempstead. These locations may not have mile-long powder runs or luxury chalets, but they do provide reliable snow conditions and enough variety to cater to skiers and snowboarders of all levels. Plus, they often come with stunning scenic views and shorter queues than their European counterparts.

So whether you're new to skiing or just want a quick weekend getaway on the slopes, these UK ski locations are definitely worth checking out.

Cairngorm Mountain Snowsports

Whether you're a seasoned skier or someone looking to try out the sport for the first time, the Cairngorm Mountain ski area in Scotland is the perfect destination for winter fun. Nestled amidst stunning natural beauty, this winter playground caters to families, adrenaline junkies, and everyone in between.

info@cairngormmountain.co.uk or +44 (0) 1479 861 261. Visit Website

Glenmore Ski & Snowboard

Glenshee Ski Centre is an alpine snowsports area in the Scottish Highlands, located above the Cairnwell Pass at the head of Glen Shee. It is Britain's largest alpine snowsports area and is referred to as the 'Scottish Three Glens'1. The ski area covers 2,000 acres and 22 lifts provide access to 25 miles of pistes.

info@ski-glenshee.info or +44 (0)13397 41320. Visit Website

Nevis Range

Nevis Range offers unforgettable adventures alongside Britain's highest mountain, Ben Nevis. The fantastic terrain caters to various levels of expertise while providing awe-inspiring views.

info@nevisrange.co.uk or +44 (0)1397 705 825. Visit Website

The Lecht 2090

Glencoe Mountain is Scotland's oldest ski resort, dating back to 1956. The ski resort offers 24.3 km of slopes for skiing and snowboarding, with 9 lifts to transport guests. The winter sports area is situated between the elevations of 360 and 1,070 m1. Glencoe has become increasingly popular in recent years among more daredevil skiers and riders, with one run in particular called the Fly Paper - the steepest black run in Britain.

info@lecht.co.uk or +44 (0)1975 651440. Visit Website
